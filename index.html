About me

I am a 5th year Ph.D. student in Prof. Jian Ma's lab in the School of Computer Science at Carnegie Mellon University. I earned a Master in Machine Learning during my Ph.D. and hold a B.E. in Computer Science and Technology from Yao Class, Institute for Interdisciplinary Information Sciences at Tsinghua University. During undergraduate, I worked with Prof. Jianyang Zeng at Tsinghua University and Prof. Jinbo Xu at Toyota Technological Institute at Chicago.

My research interests lie at the interface of deep learning and biology. I design neural networks and probabilistic graphical models for biology problems, including protein-protein interactions, spatial transcriptomics, and single-cell 3D genome structures.
